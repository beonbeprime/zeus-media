# Padrão de prompt de motion (image-to-video) - PHOTO LYRICS

Regra de ouro: movimento SUTIL que nunca tira a legibilidade do texto.
O texto permanece 100% do tempo no quadro. Duração 4-5s. Prompt em inglês.
PROIBIDO: rotação rápida, transição interna, pessoas cruzando na frente do
texto, qualquer instrução que reescreva/anime/morfe as letras.

| Categoria da cena | Prompt de motion |
|---|---|
| Garrafa/objeto sobre mesa | `slow orbital camera move around the bottle, subtle zoom in, condensation drips sliding, background bokeh lights flicker, text stays sharp and centered` |
| Tatuagem/pele | `camera slowly pushes in toward the tattoo, gentle breathing motion of the skin, soft hair movement, no body rotation` |
| Placa de rua/outdoor | `static sign, cars passing with motion blur in the background, heat haze, subtle handheld camera sway` |
| Praia/areia | `gentle waves rolling in the background, sea breeze moving hair and fabric, slow tilt up, written text in the sand untouched by the water` |
| Parede grafitada/muro | `slow lateral dolly along the wall, pedestrian shadow passing, pigeons in the background, paint texture catching the light` |
| Letreiro neon | `neon flicker and glow pulsing softly, light rain falling, reflections shimmering on the wet ground, locked camera` |
| Papel/bilhete/livro/agenda | `soft candlelight flickering, page corner lifting slightly with the breeze, dust particles floating, slow zoom in` |
| Espelho/vidro | `steam slowly drifting, droplets running down the glass around the written words, camera static` |
| Café/drink | `steam rising from the cup, light swirl at the foam edges without breaking the lettering, slow top-down push in` |
| Tecido/camiseta | `fabric moving gently with the breeze, person breathing naturally, camera slowly pushing in, lettering stays flat and readable` |

Rodapé obrigatório em todo prompt: `subtle, slow, cinematic, 5 seconds,
the written text must remain perfectly legible and unchanged`.
